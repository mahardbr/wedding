The Story of Us

We met in high school, where we both attended Sherburne-Earlville, in the same grade - class of 2012. In those days, we didn't cross paths too much, and were in very different cliques. Our story does not really start until the very first moments of the year 2017, when Ceridwen's friend, Shannon, coerced Ceridwen's sister (their DD), Bronwen, to stop off at the local bar in Earlville - The Huff Brau - on their way home from a night of revelry in celebration of the New Year. The bar was full of Sherburne-Earlville natives, and after a few minutes, Ceridwen sat down next to Randy at the bar and struck up a conversation. For what seemed like hours, they caught up on everything that had been going on in their lives since high school ended, where they were going to college, etc. The night drew to a close, goodbyes were said, and everyone went their separate ways. Ceridwen and Randy would regularly chat on snapchat, but nothing more.

The First "Date"
A few months of consistent texting and snapchatting passed, and finally the two were both going to be home from college for Easter. Ceridwen making the journey from Albany, where she was finishing up grad school, and Randy from Buffalo where he was working. Ceridwen agreed to meet up with Randy in Hamilton along the way home, first for drinks at the Hour Glass, and then a quick meal at La Iguana. Of course, conversation came naturally and they had a wonderful time while having some drinks. Then at dinner, a beautiful bouquet of flowers arrived at the table for Ceridwen and she realized that this was not just any old hang out, and Randy seemed quite the romantic.
