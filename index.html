Directions


Hotel

We have a block of rooms at the Comfort Inn and Suites in Medina, NY. Unfortunately, this is a small hotel with a limited number of rooms so please book as soon as possible using the group name Mahardy/Briggs.

In the event that all rooms are full at Comfort Inn and Suites, we recommend La Quinta Inn & Suites by Wyndham Batavia. There is no block reserved for this hotel, and shuttle service will not be available - we’re sorry 😢. This hotel is about 20 minutes from the venue.

Transportation

There will be a bus making two trips from The Comfort Inn & Suites in Medina to the venue departing around 3pm, with a second bus departing around 3:50pm.
Return buses are scheduled to bring guests back to the hotel around 10:10, 11 & 11:30pm (Event ends at 11).
It is discouraged by the venue to leave cars overnight, so if you plan on drinking please make every effort to get on one of the two busses in the afternoon. Yard games will be set up for entertainment prior to the ceremony starting at 4:30 for any early arrivers.

Gifts

Since we’ve been sharing our lives together for the past four years, we already have all the household items that we need. In lieu of traditional gifts, monetary gifts are graciously accepted.
